Iowa State University

Iowa State University of Science and Technology is a public land grant university located in Ames, Iowa. Founded in 1858, Iowa State University has a long tradition of higher education. In 1942, Iowa State professor John Atanasoff and his assistant invented the first electronic digital computer.
